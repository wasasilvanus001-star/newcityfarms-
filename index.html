About New City Farm

New City Farms Nig. Ltd. is a forward-thinking agricultural company committed to producing high-quality crops through sustainable farming practices and modern agricultural techniques. Our passion for excellence drives us to cultivate healthy, nutritious, and environmentally responsible produce that contributes to food security and supports economic growth.

With a strong focus on innovation, integrity, and quality, we specialize in the cultivation of maize, rice, beans, groundnuts, and soya beans etc. Every stage of our farming process from land preparation and planting to harvesting and distribution is carried out with professionalism and attention to detail.

At New City Farms, we believe agriculture is more than growing crops; it is about creating value, empowering communities, and building a sustainable future. We remain dedicated to providing dependable agricultural solutions while establishing lasting relationships with our customers, partners, and stakeholders.

Processed Products

Luscious Rice

Luscious Rice is New City Farm's premium parboiled rice brand, carefully selected from high-quality paddy and processed to deliver exceptional taste, cleanliness, and nutritional value. Each grain is professionally cleaned, stone-free, and hygienically packaged to maintain its freshness from our farm to your table. Whether you're preparing everyday family meals or special occasions, Luscious Rice cooks evenly, has an appealing aroma, and delivers a delicious taste that keeps customers coming back. Available in different bag sizes, it is suitable for households, restaurants, hotels, supermarkets, and bulk buyers seeking dependable quality.
